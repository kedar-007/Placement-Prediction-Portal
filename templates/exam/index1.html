Our Algorithm

Logistic regression is a popular machine learning algorithm used for binary classification tasks, which means it is well-suited for predicting outcomes that have two possible values, such as whether a student will be placed or not placed in a job. In the context of placement prediction, logistic regression can be applied to analyze various features or factors associated with a student's profile to determine the likelihood of placement.

Aptitude Test

Aptitude tests are assessments designed to measure a person's abilities and skills in various areas, such as numerical reasoning, verbal reasoning, logical reasoning, and abstract reasoning. These tests aim to evaluate an individual's cognitive capabilities and their potential for success in different domains, including education and employment.
